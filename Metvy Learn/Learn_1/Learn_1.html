Metvy, India’s first AI-driven, multilingual,
business networking app , believes that
true knowledge comes from authentic
connections, their experiences and
guidance. We aim to provide a solid
platform for these online professional
networks to grow and foster a community
built on intelligent learning and preeminent
expertise.
As a value addition to our vision, we conceptualized
METVY LEARN, a concept in the form of a program
that cultivates innovation, entrepreneurship,
intercompany and intercultural exchange through
professional networking . It focuses on authentic
learning that comes from real life experiences of
Entrepreneurs, Academicians, Industrialists and
other working professionals from various reputed
institutions such as Bloomberg, Facebook, ISB,
Walmart and PepsiCo amongst others and from
diverse backgrounds to achieve a holistic
augmentationof knowledge.
