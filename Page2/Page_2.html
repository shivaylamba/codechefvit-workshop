Metvy is an extremely user-friendly and free
professional networking app that can be used
by anyone and everyone.

All you need to do is follow these steps:

- Sign-up on the Metvy App.

- Complete your user profile.

The AI takes this input and draws out relations
between different sets of skills, job titles,
experience, age and requirements of the people.
Further, it classifies the people on the app based
on these relations, to group together similarly
minded people and those people who can
connect instantly with each other.

- Meet your connections and build your business network in India .

- Find relevant jobs or internship opportunities .

- Get your career rolling.
