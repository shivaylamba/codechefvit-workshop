Metvy is a real-time hyperlocal, multilingual,
professional networking app for individuals and
organizations that predicts your networking
needs and helps you connect with relevant people.
It aims at utilizing the infinite human potential
present hyper-locally through an automated, intuitive
platform that provides need-based networking
where people can find like-minded people around
them. Using geospatial trends, the app sends you a
notification whenever you come in contact with any
other user in your vicinity who is fulfilling your needs.
This way, you can connect with the right person, at
the right time.

Be it job opportunities, investor meetings or even
getting a cup of coffee with a person of similar interests,

Metvy is the single stop for it all.



Metvy is being backed by the Govt of Delhi and is being
run by Y Combinator Startup School alumni, along with
alumni and students of institutes like IIMs, IITs, St. Stephen’s,
SRCC, Miranda House, Kirori Mal, Gargi and many others.
It has been named as one of the best upcoming apps by
Times App Search and has received appreciation from
RedBull Basement, TIE Delhi apart from many other
national institutions.
